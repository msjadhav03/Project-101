स्वामी ओम प्रकाश सरस्वती

Swami Om Prakash Saraswati

Swamiji, as he was lovingly called, was sent by Osho to start a Centre in Delhi in 1978. With total dedication and surrender, he established the Osho Rajyoga Meditation Centre which is the fountain head of all meditation activities in the Capital Region of Delhi.
